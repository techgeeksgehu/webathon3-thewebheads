Rules and Eligibility

  • 1.Team Formation: Participants can form teams of 2 to 5 members.
  • 2.Project Scope: Teams can work on any project within the given theme or problem statement.
  • 3.Time Limit: The Hackathon will have a specific time limit.
  • 4.The use of pre-existing code or projects is not allowed, except for open-source libraries or frameworks.
  • 5. The Hackathon will offer prizes or awards to winning teams based on the judging criteria.
  • 6.In case of any disputes or concerns, the decision of the organizers and judging panel will be final.
  • 7.Liability: The organizers and sponsors of the Hackathon hold no liability for any damages, losses, or injuries incurred during the event. Participants are responsible for their own safety, equipment, and actions.
  • 8.Changes to the Rules: Organizers reserve the right to make changes to the Hackathon rules, format, or prizes at any time. Any modifications will be communicated to the participants in a clear and timely manner.
  • 9.Consent and Media Release: Participants may be required to provide consent for their photographs, videos, or project details to be used by the organizers for promotional or media purposes.
  • Note: There will be three (First, Second and Third) prizes in hardware and three (First, Second and Third) prizes in software category. Decision of the evaluation panel members based on the performance of the team will be final. There will not be any prizes problem statement wise.
